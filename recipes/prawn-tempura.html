Fried Shrimp Tempura Recipe

Description

A japanese dish when served is crispy and flavourful. Perfect as a side dish or finger snack.

Ingredients

  • 10 jumbo sized shrimps deshelled shrimps
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cornstarch
  • 1 egg
  • Ice cold water

Steps

    Step 1: Prepare shrimp
  1. Deshell shrimps if needed
  2. Cut a few lines along the underside of the shrimp, this is so it doesn't curl up while cooking
  3. Mix the shrimps in cornstarch
    Step 2: Batter
  1. Crack open and mix egg with icy cold water
  2. Add all-purpose flour and mix it gently (not too thorough)
    Step 3 :Fry
  1. Preheat oil in deep fryer/wok/pan to 170 degrees - 180 degrees
  2. Fry the shrimp in batches to ensure even cooking and temperature control for about 4-5mins flipping if needed.
  3. Remove and serve.
